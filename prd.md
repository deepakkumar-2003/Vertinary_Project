Product Requirements Document (PRD)
1. Product Overview
Product Name: Advanced Veterinary Clinic, Dairy & Farm Management System
Product Type: Web-based Application
Target Users: Veterinarians, Clinic Staff, Farm Owners, Dairy Managers, Admin Users

1.1 Purpose
The purpose of this system is to digitize and centralize veterinary clinic operations, animal healthcare records, dairy and farm management, financial records, and reporting. The system reduces manual paperwork, improves data accuracy, and enables real-time decision-making.

1.2 Goals & Objectives
Digitize animal healthcare and farm records

Improve efficiency of veterinarians and clinic staff

Track vaccinations, treatments, AI records, and reminders

Manage dairy production, loans, insurance, and claims

Provide accurate reports for health, dairy, and finance

2. Stakeholders
Veterinary Doctors

Clinic Reception & Staff

Farm Owners

Dairy Managers

System Administrator

Management / Owners

3. User Roles & Access Control
Role	Description	Access Level
Admin	System configuration and full control	Full Access
Veterinarian	Medical records and treatments	Medical Modules
Staff	Data entry and scheduling	Limited Access
Farm Owner	View farm, animal, and dairy data	Read / Limited Write
4. Functional Requirements (Modules)
4.1 User & Security Module
Secure login with username & password

Role-based access control

Password reset & session management

Activity logs

4.2 Customer Management Module
Store customer details (name, contact, address)

Emergency contact information

Customer search & update

4.3 Animal Registration Module
Register animals with unique ID

Store breed, age, gender, ownership

Animal history tracking

4.4 Disease & Diagnosis Module
Record diseases and symptoms

Upload disease images

Diagnosis history per animal

4.5 Treatment Management Module
Store prescribed treatments

Medicine details and dosage

Treatment timelines

4.6 Vaccination Management Module
Track vaccination schedules

Automatic reminders & alerts

Due date tracking

4.7 Artificial Insemination (AI) Module
Record AI procedures

Track checkups and due dates

Pregnancy confirmation records

4.8 Milk Dairy Management Module
Daily milk production records

Animal-wise and date-wise tracking

Dairy summary reports

4.9 Loan Management Module
Loan details per customer/farm

Due date tracking

Loan status management

4.10 Insurance & Claim Module
Store insurance policy details

Claim records and status

Document upload support

4.11 Report Module
Health reports

Dairy production reports

Financial & loan reports

Export reports (PDF/Excel)

5. Non-Functional Requirements
5.1 Performance
System should handle multiple users simultaneously

Fast search and retrieval of records

5.2 Security
Encrypted passwords

Secure authentication

Data access based on roles

5.3 Usability
Simple and intuitive UI

Mobile and desktop friendly

Minimal training required

5.4 Scalability
Support growing number of animals and records

Future module expansion

5.5 Availability
99% uptime

Backup and recovery support

6. Assumptions & Constraints
Internet connectivity required

Users have basic computer knowledge

Initial deployment for small to medium clinics

7. Success Metrics
Reduction in manual paperwork

Improved record accuracy

Timely vaccination & AI reminders

User satisfaction

8. Future Enhancements

AI-based disease prediction
